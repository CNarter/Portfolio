My Projects

Worthing Street Game

The Worthing Street Game is a fun, interactive street game, playable on any mobile device, showcasing interesting historical and architectural places in Worthing. The objective is to create an enjoyable game playable over a small, accessible, mapped area in central Worthing. It includes 10-15 interesting facts, views, architectural, or historical places that are always visible and not dependent on seasons, weather, or regular changes/closures. The 'journey' is on foot, ensuring accessibility for most people. Future monetization via sponsorship or pay-per-play is being considered in the build and design, though making money is currently excluded from the project's scope.

Expected delivery for the game is Summer 2026.

Key Features:

  • Interactive map with a flat, accessible route, including the museum, pier, and cinema, with no steps and many crossings.
  • Location-based puzzles and clues designed around permanent landmarks in Worthing.
  • Examples of clues include: "With many seats, but no legs to walk. Over the sea but will never sail away." (Worthing Pier ).
  • Another clue involves inspecting "pretty panels on the pier" for an iconic London Transport bus to find a number.
  • Future potential for 'side quest' add-ons and monetization via sponsorship or pay-per-play.
  • Designed to be inclusive and accessible, including features for disabled access and 'changing places' toilets, and can be played on foot.

My Contributions:

This project is being run solely by me, giving me full control, flexible deadlines, and agility in responding to changes. My contributions span all aspects of the project lifecycle:

  • **Project Management:** Managed the entire project independently, logging all hours worked against planned future income using a time-tracker in Monday (my PM tool). Created a dummy budget plan to demonstrate a structured approach.
  • **Content Creation:** Defined the route, photographed "answers" to clues, and wrote clues and solutions.
  • **Mapping & Accessibility:** Created a mapped area considering accessibility, ensuring a flat and inclusive route.
  • **Stakeholder Management:** Identified and engaged with key stakeholders including Adur & Worthing Council, Creative Waves, Town Center Bid Association, local businesses like time4Tech, and the Selden Residents Association.
  • **Risk Management:** Conducted a comprehensive risk analysis, identifying potential issues such as changes to the ActionBound application, poor connectivity, and user interest, with mitigation plans in place.
  • **Strategic Planning:** Conducted a SWOT analysis, highlighting strengths like individual project control and existing knowledge of Worthing, and identifying opportunities for future AI integration and monetization.
  • **Monitoring & Evaluation:** Planned to build a shareable webpage, create social media channels, set up monitoring via GA and HotJar, and collect feedback via Typeform to measure success against gameplay and sponsorship goals.

Technologies & Tools Used:

ActionBound (anticipated platform), Monday (PM tool), Google Analytics, HotJar, Typeform
